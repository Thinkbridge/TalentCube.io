It’s for parents & coaches. It’s for young athletes… and Grandpa too.

It brings together all of the moments that bind teams together. It shares and stores the stories and moments that matter. It’s friendship. It’s sportsmanship.

It puts schedules, scores, videos, conversation, photos, and a visual roster into one beautiful package. And it’s a whole lotta fun. 

Features That Matter

The Reasons You’ll Love It

SAVE TIME

Instantly push the important stuff to people’s mobile devices (e.g. practice is canceled or postponed).

CLEAN UP YOUR INBOX

Get rid of lengthy & messy e-mail chains, reply-all e-mails and the dreaded mass response of “thanks!” and “ok!”

ORGANIZE WHAT'S IMPORTANT

On Three communicate time & place in an over-abundance of ways. Need one-touch directions to the game? Yeah, we got that.

THE BEST THINGS IN LIFE ARE FREE

Youth Sports can be expensive. We know. So what’s the best part of On Three? It’s FREE. FOREVER. No per-person or per-team fees.

THE PERFECT PLACE TO SHARE

Post team photos & videos. Collect & curate moments & memories.

MAKE SOCIALIZING EASIER

“Remember” Devyn’s dad’s name. Put names & faces together. Connect better with parents & coaches, friends & family.

All Your Teams • One Content Stream

Post Content

Message the team about a lost glove. Send alerts so that everyone knows that parking is crazy at the fields. Congratulate the team for a game well played.

Post Photos

Every game someone grabs a great photo of the action. Easily post photos wit captions right inside the stream.

Post Videos

You just became team parent of the week because you filmed the game-winning shot. Get that highlight out to the whole team by posting it inside the stream.

Team Rosters Visualized

Track Every Team • Any Sport  – Create a new team. Connect Players & Parents. See coaches, parents, players and fans all in one place. Manage 3 kids, 2 sports, on 3 teams. Team pages let you see who’s on the team and if you’re a coach, who can do what. 

Connect Outside Practices & Games – Whether the coach needs extra help at practice or you’re arranging a play date, everyone on the team can display there contact info for the rest of the team. Handy.

Every Event. Every Team. Up to date.

  • Schedule one event or a whole season.

Who Are We

We’re parents, coaches, and players just like you.

And we believe sports should inspire kids. Sports should be fun. They shouldn’t be full of “reply all emails,” missed events, or Grandparents in Arizona who need 30 minutes of explanation on how to see a photo e-mailed to them.

On Three’s mission is to create a greater sense of belonging in youth sports. Taking the job of connecting people and enhancing it with technology. Big. We know. But that’s what it’s all about isn’t it? Because if you can do that, then focusing on the kids and having fun become the norm, not how-are-we-going-to-get-there and who’s-bringing-the-drinks. And that might even lead to stuff like helping kids learn life lessons, build values, and establish confidence in themselves.
